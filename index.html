My story

Before serving people in real estate, we served communities in South America as missionaries. I even considered becoming a Counselor at one point. You see, we have a passion for helping people. We love listening to their life-changing stories, and those interested in buying a home are always dealing with events that change their lives.

When we made the dive into real estate we knew it would be a good fit, but we didn't realize just how good. Within a few months, we were performing among the top agents in the area - even with 5 little ones at home. It all comes down to listening, above-and-beyond service, and clear communication.
